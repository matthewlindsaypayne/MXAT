Bio

Matthew most recently finished playing Marcel in “Thirteen Dead Husbands” (Cohesion Theater Company), for which he received The Bad Oracle’s March 2015 “Actor Stealing the Spotlight.” Previous credits also include Sebastian in Milburn Stone Theatre's "Twelfth Night," James in Single Carrot's "The Pairing Formerly Known As Friends," and Pozzo in Loyola University Maryland's "Waiting for Godot." Matthew is an avid stage combat enthusiast training under Lewis Shaw, and a student of "Kunst des Fechtens," training under Vince Hann.
